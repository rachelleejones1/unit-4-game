Crystal Collector

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four different crystals. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.


To win the game, you must make your total score equal to the exact value of the random number. If your total score exceeds the value of the random number, you lose.


The value of each crystal changes when the game restarts. To know the value, you must click on the crystal.
